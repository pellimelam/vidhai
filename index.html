About Vidhwaan Group

Vidhwaan Group is an international technology company building platforms in artificial intelligence, software engineering, cultural preservation, and digital infrastructure. It operates globally across multiple domains including AI tools, developer ecosystems, and cultural platforms.

Founder of Vidhwaan Group

Nithin Gidigi is the founder of Vidhwaan Group, a technology and cultural ecosystem focused on making technology accessible and preserving traditional knowledge systems. His work spans social impact, education, software development, and cultural platforms.
